About Us

Welcome to INFINITY SOLUTIONS

Infinity Solutions is one of the finest firms in Assam, one spot solution to all the laboratory requirements. The firm was established eight years back in 2016, with a meagrely financial turnouts. A year later, Infinity Solutions turned around the tables with new customer friendly strategies, focused mainly on timely deliveries, unmatched Services and ultimate customer satisfaction. These strategic moves were not finance centric, but were mainly for customer satisfaction. This customer satisfaction brought as a byproduct good financial turnouts, Which helped Infinity Solutions to have strong foothold in the market. Now the firm is so well established that it serves to all big Pharma, food industries and prime institutions in Assam. Infinity solutions deals with all kinds of laboratory, chemicals, glassware, Lab equipments/instruments, bulk solvent, and every other kind of miscellaneous laboratory requirements. Firm is committed to provide the best possible services, irrespective of profit margins or order values. Every single customer is pride of our firm and will be served with dignity always.
