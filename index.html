About Us

Welcome to INFINITY SOLUTIONS

Infinity Solutions is one of the finest firms in Assam, one STOP solution FOR all the laboratory requirements. The firm was established eight years back in 2016 by its sole proprietor Mrs. Nabamita Chatterjee, with MEAGRE financial turnouts. A year later, Infinity Solutions turned ROUND the tables with new customer friendly strategies, focused mainly on timely deliveries, unmatched services and ultimate customer satisfaction. These strategic moves were not finance centric, but were mainly for customer satisfaction. This customer satisfaction WAS brought as a byproduct OF good financial turnouts, which helped Infinity Solutions to have A strong foothold in the market. Now the firm is so well established that it serves all big Pharma, Food Industries and Prime Institutions in Assam. Infinity solutions deals with all kinds of laboratory chemicals, glasswares, plastic wares, Lab EQUIPMENT/instruments, bulk solvents, and ALL other kindS of miscellaneous laboratory requirements. THE Firm is committed to provide the best possible services, irrespective of profit margins or order values. Every single customer is THE pride of our firm and WILL ALWAYS BE SERVED WITH DIGNITY.
